Student Clubs


1. Creative Computing Club (C3)


This club encourages students to actively participate in computing related activities over and above their curriculum. Students get a chance to learn various other prevailing programming languages, different designing software, coding techniques, networking etc. This club utilizes the services of Computer Science Faculty and organizes various interactive sessions and workshops to get hands-on experience on different platforms and software. This Club takes the responsibility in designing the Student Hand Book and the College Magazine “COLLEGE BUZZ”.


2. Literary Club


Its objective is to inculcate literacy tastes, to improve oratorical, communication and sublime skills. This club utilizes the services of the English Faculty and conducts series of events like debates, GD’s throughout the year and also brings out the College Magazine - "COLLEGE BUZZ" which shall be released at College Day Event. Many Students of this society are also the members of TOASTMASTERS club.
